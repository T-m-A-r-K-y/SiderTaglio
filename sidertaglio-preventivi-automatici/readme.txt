*WooCrypt:* Accept cryptocurrencies seamlessly on your WooCommerce store. *No upfront costs*, only *0.1%* transaction fees, *wide cryptocurrency support*, *custom cryptocurrency* integration and *multi-chain* support.

== Description ==

WooCrypt, the WooCommerce plugin designed to seamlessly integrate cryptocurrency payments into your online business.

Here's WooCrypt's main features:

**Zero Initial Cost**: Say goodbye to the burden of hefty setup fees. WooCrypt is your gateway to accepting cryptocurrencies without any upfront expenses. Whether you're a startup or a well-established business, you can harness the power of digital currencies without breaking the bank.

**Unbeatable Transaction Fees**: We proudly offer the lowest transaction fees on the market – a mere 0.1%. With WooCrypt, you can maximize your revenue by minimizing transaction costs. Keep more of your hard-earned money where it belongs.

**Multi-Chain Compatibility**: WooCrypt currently supports Ethereum and Binance Smart Chain (BSC), with plans to expand to even more blockchain networks. Our commitment to multi-chain compatibility ensures that you can cater to a diverse customer base, making your store accessible to cryptocurrency enthusiasts worldwide.

**Wide Cryptocurrency Support**: WooCrypt doesn't limit your customers to a single coin. It supports all major cryptocurrencies on each integrated blockchain. Whether your customers prefer Bitcoin, Ethereum, or any other popular digital asset, they can make payments with ease.

**Custom Token Integration**: Tailor your payment options to match your customers' preferences. WooCrypt allows you to add custom tokens, giving your customers the flexibility to pay with their preferred digital assets.

**Wallet Compatibility**: Your customers can use their favorite cryptocurrency wallets to make payments, ensuring a seamless and secure transaction process. WooCrypt supports all major cryptocurrency wallets, providing peace of mind to both you and your customers.

**Fast and Secure**: WooCrypt ensures swift and secure cryptocurrency transactions. Your customers can shop with confidence, knowing their data and funds are protected by blockchain technology.

**Full WooCommerce Integration**: WooCrypt seamlessly integrates with your WooCommerce store, making it easy to manage payments, track orders, and provide a smooth shopping experience. Your customers won't even notice the difference in the checkout process – except for the added convenience of cryptocurrency payments.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What cryptocurrencies does WooCrypt support? =

WooCrypt supports all major cryptocurrencies on each integrated blockchain. This includes but is not limited to Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Ripple (XRP), and more. It also allows for the addition of custom tokens.

= What kind of customer support does WooCrypt provide? =

WooCrypt offers dedicated customer support to assist with any questions or issues you may encounter during integration or use. Contact our support team for prompt assistance.
